Super Bowl hype will only grow, and the actual game will only shrink - Yahoo Sports

 The Super Bowl, a cultural extravaganza, attracts millions with overwhelming hype, costing $7 million per 30-second ad. Amidst glitz 
 and ads, football plays a minor role. Though overshadowed by hype, the game itself can be sublime. The event's 
 massive scale is an attention arms race.
